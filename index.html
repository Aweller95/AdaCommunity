A student led initiative

We are a new college focused on preparing our students for the digital future, by equipping them with what they need to make valuable contributions to society through technology. As a part of our vision, we wish to provide services to the local community, offering to carry out tech-related services the community may require, free of charge. We will be running workshops for all different sections of the local community. So if you have something you needed help with or simply wanted to have a chat, send us a request!
